Same Old Story

Answer: INCA
by David Berardo


Solvers may initially notice 'TOY STORY' in the top left of the grid, which might correspond to the clue "1. Talking Trinkets", indicating that the clues refer to movies in some way.

Another movie in the top row is "The Two Towers", however it bends in the grid. The corresponding clue for this entry is "2. Ring Friend Quest", where the 2 refers to the second movie in the Lord of the Rings Franchise. Each clue refers to a movie series, with the number indicating the movie in that series.

As per the flavor text "...they keep adding twists...", each movie is entered into the grid with N bends, where N is one less than the movie order (i.e. the 2nd movie added one twist, the 3rd movie added two twists etc). The table below lists the answers for each clue:

FREE MASON!!

Answer: THE GROOM
by Michael Calzadilla & Kaley Brauer

The title, along with the flavor text, hint towards using the pig pen cypher (also known as the freemason cipher) to decode the symbols, with animals standing in for dots.

If we try to decode them going clockwise starting from the top left, we get the following string:

WHIAGTIST NDEAQPGEP GEEIUBJAV EXEHHHCHI

Which doesn't quite look right. The first thing to notice is that the animal heads rotate as we go along the edge, indicating that we should re-orient the image when we turn to a new side. Interpreting the symbols in this way (which leaves the top row unchanged) gives:

WHIAGTIST NHEPORINR CEEATHRIS EWEDDDING

This still doesn't look quite right, although we have a lot less Q's and X's which seems promising. If we read the letters corresponding only to the red fish, they spell out:

IGNORE RED

Telling us that we should ignore the red herrings that have been leading us astray! The remaining letters spell out:

WHAT IS THE PRINCE AT HIS WEDDING

The enumeration tells us the final answer is a 3 and a 5 letter word, and the phrase which fits the clue is THE GROOM, our final answer.

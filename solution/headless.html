At first the answers to the clues seems ambiguous, i.e. "Month". The title of the puzzle, as well as the phrase "clean and lean cut" in the flavor text hint at the fact the answers must all be words which can have have their first letter removed while still making a valid word:

The next piece of flavor text tells us to "sort out the remains", so we sort the words by the second letter, which is what remains after we 'behead' the words:


(M)ARCH
(A)CUTE
(R)ICE
(R)OAR
(O)PEN

Their heads spell out the word MARROW, and the flavor text tells us to look for whats hidden inside their heads. Applying the transformation one more time, if we remove the first letter we're left with the solution:


(M)ARROW -> ARROW
